# RISK & ISSUE LOG

### How to use

- Log anything that can block delivery or harm quality.
- Keep entries short. Focus on mitigation and ownership.

| Risk / Issue | Impact | Probability | Mitigation | Owner | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Example: Payment provider approval may take longer than planned | High | Medium | Submit application in Week 2 and prepare fallback provider | @Bao Dinh | Open |

### Status legend

- Open
- Monitoring
- Mitigated
- Closed
