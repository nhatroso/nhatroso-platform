**Product Vision**

## Vision Statement

Build a comprehensive digital boarding-house management platform that helps **landlords automate operations, ensure financial transparency, and optimize revenue**, while providing **tenants with a convenient, clear, and trustworthy experience for payments and bill tracking**.

The system leverages **OCR technology and a modern microservices architecture** to replace manual record-keeping, reduce errors, save time, and raise the professionalism of boarding-house management in Vietnam.

---

## Target Users

- **Small-to-mid sized landlords / boarding-house operators** who need a centralized tool to automate utilities, invoicing, and payments.
- **Tenants** who want transparent charges, fast payment, and easy contract tracking.

---

## Core Value Proposition

The product delivers three core values:

1. **Operational automation**
    - Meter photo capture → reading recognition → billing calculation → invoice delivery → payment confirmation.
2. **Transparency and fewer disputes**
    - Invoices include photo evidence, clear history, and automated reconciliation.
3. **Better business outcomes for landlords**
    - Less admin time, higher occupancy, and improved cash flow.

---

## Long-term Vision (3–5 years)

- Become **the most widely used boarding-house management platform in Vietnam** for the small-to-mid landlord segment.
- Expand into:
    - Serviced apartments and mini condominiums
    - AI-driven analytics for electricity and water consumption
    - IoT integration for automated meter reading
    - A broader ecosystem for payments and rental services
