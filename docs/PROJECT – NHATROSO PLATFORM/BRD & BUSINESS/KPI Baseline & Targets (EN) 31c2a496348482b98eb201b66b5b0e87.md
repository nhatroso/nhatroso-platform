# 2️⃣ Short version

### KPI Baseline & Target

**Baseline:**

- Occupancy: 65%
- On-time payment: 60%
- Utility disputes: 15%
- Utility closing time: 3–4 hours

**Targets after system implementation:**

- Occupancy ≥ 85%
- On-time payment ≥ 90%
- Disputes ≤ 2%
- Closing time ≤ 30 minutes

→ Used to **evaluate business success of the system**.

# 3️⃣ Writing rules

Principles:

- KPIs must be **quantifiable**
- Include a **pre-project baseline**
- Include a **target and a time horizon**
