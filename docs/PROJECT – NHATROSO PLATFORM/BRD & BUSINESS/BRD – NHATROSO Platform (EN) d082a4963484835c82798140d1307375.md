**BUSINESS REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (BRD)**

**Project: NHATROSO PLATFORM – Boarding House Management System with automatic electricity and water meter number capture from photos**

### 1. Project Overview

#### 1.1 Project Objectives

Build a software system to **automate boarding house management**, including:

- Centralized management of rooms, rental contracts, and residents
- Capture electricity and water meter readings from photos
- Automatic invoice generation and support for online payments
- Revenue reporting and room status or occupancy reporting

The ultimate goal is to **reduce manual work, increase transparency, and optimize operations** for landlords.

#### 1.2 Current Problems

Manual management processes cause:

- Errors when recording electricity and water readings
- Disputes due to lack of photo evidence
- Data scattered across multiple tools
- Late payments that affect cash flow

These limitations reduce the efficiency of boarding house management.

#### 1.3 Project Scope

**In scope**

- Room, resident, and rental contract management
- Automatically extract meter numbers from photos
- Invoice generation and QR payment
- Revenue dashboard

**Out of scope**

- IoT integration for automatic meter reading
- Full support for all electronic meter types
- Large-scale commercial deployment

These boundaries are clearly stated in the project outline.

### 3. Business Objectives (SMART)

- Automate meter reading to **reduce monthly processing time**
- Improve transparency to **reduce disputes between landlords and tenants**
- Improve cash flow with **online payment and automatic status updates**
- Increase management efficiency with **real-time revenue reporting**

These objectives come from the need for digital transformation in boarding house management.

### 4. Current Process (AS-IS)

Current process:

1. Landlord visits each room and records meter readings manually
2. Calculates costs manually or in Excel
3. Sends payment notifications manually
4. Waits for bank transfer reconciliation

Pain points:

- Time-consuming and error-prone
- Lack of transparency
- Hard to consolidate data

This is the main driver of the project.

### 5. Future Process (TO-BE)

After implementation:

1. Take a meter photo → the system extracts the number automatically
2. System calculates charges and creates an invoice
3. Sends notification + QR code for payment
4. Automatically confirms payment when funds are received
5. Shows real-time revenue reports

The new process **automates the full invoice lifecycle**.

### 8. Assumptions & Constraints

**Assumptions**

- Users have smartphones and internet access
- Common mechanical meters are used

**Constraints**

- 4-month implementation timeline
- Meter number extraction depends on photo quality

Risks and limitations are clearly identified.
